About ACM Wilmington

Welcome to our Computing Club! We are a community of passionate individuals who share a love for technology and innovation. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, our club offers a welcoming environment where members can collaborate on projects, learn new skills, and dive into the world of computing. From exploring programming languages and software development to discussing the latest trends in artificial intelligence and cybersecurity, there's always something exciting happening here. Our club organizes workshops, hackathons, and guest speaker events, providing opportunities to develop both practical and theoretical knowledge. It's a great place to network, build real-world projects, and even prepare for future careers in tech. Join us to grow your skills, make lasting connections, and be part of a supportive community that values curiosity, creativity, and learning!
